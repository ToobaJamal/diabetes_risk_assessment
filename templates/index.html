Diabetes Risk Assessment

Diabetes is a health condition in which body does not make good use of insulin released by pancreas, resulting in higher blood sugar levels. High blood sugar levels can cause complicated health issues like heart disease, kidney disease, and vision loss.

According to Centers for Disease Control and Prevention, more than 37 million American adults have diabetes and about 1 in 5 people with diabetes don't know they have it.

This diabetes risk assessment is aimed at predicting early stage type-2 diabetes risk in individuals of age 20-90.

The assessment is easy, requires a few minutes to complete and provides you an instant prediction.
